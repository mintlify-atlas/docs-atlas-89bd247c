## Pricing structure

Our plans are priced based on your redirect volume and feature requirements. Each plan includes:

### Redirect limits

- **Maximum redirects**: The total number of domain redirects you can create
- **Analytics events per redirect**: Monthly analytics events tracked per redirect
- **Rules per redirect**: Number of intelligent routing rules you can configure
- **Analytics retention**: How long we store your analytics data

## Billing

Pricing is billed based on the interval specified in your plan (monthly or annually). All plans include:

- **Transparent pricing**: No hidden fees or surprise charges
- **Flexible billing**: Choose between monthly or annual billing
- **Secure payments**: Industry-standard payment processing
- **Easy upgrades**: Seamlessly upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime
